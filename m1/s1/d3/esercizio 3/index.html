Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds this festive fofaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN
Fresh Cranerry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) inkewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping;

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon nalsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a parking knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

the dough ingredientds until the flour is fully absirbed. You don't need to actually knead the
dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour...
